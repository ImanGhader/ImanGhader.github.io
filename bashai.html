Bashai Package Design

Package Design • Research

Bashai is a specialty tea package designed to serve guests in a formal environment. I was inspired by the tea culture after a recent trip to Morocco, and decided to base the design concept on the personal and social experience of serving and drinking tea.

I chose to make the package resemble the traditional Moroccan teacup to evoke a familiar user interaction. Also, wrapping the individual teabags with elegant packaging is meant to personalize the experience. Luxurious hotels, formal parties and fine dining experiences are examples of where Bashai would best fit in as a brand.

The brand name combines two Arabic words: Basha and Shai. Basha translates to ‘Master’ (of ceremony), while Shai simply means ‘Tea’. The choice of those words and their combination portrays a sense of elegance and mastery of craft as the brand speaks to a sophisticated audience.
