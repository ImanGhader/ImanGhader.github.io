Frizy Juice Campaign Design

Illustration • Campaign design

Frizy is a fictional brand for a glass-bottled sparkling juice. I created an illustration-based campaign to promote three of its flavors: orange, grape, and raspberry. Working with the theme ‘nature contained’, the concept was to bring each flavor to life by illustrating in its natural botanical origins. To stay true to the theme, I worked with a vibrant color palette and infused a watercolor look to enhance the brand’s organic and fresh image.
